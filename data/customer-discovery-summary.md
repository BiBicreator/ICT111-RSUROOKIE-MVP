# Customer Discovery Summary

## Project Title

RSU Campus Buddy (Smart Campus Chatbot for Student Services with Image Recognition)

## Target Users Studied

* Freshmen (1st-year students)
* International students
* Current RSU students

## Number of Responses / Interviews

14 survey responses and 5 interviews.

## Main Repeated Problem

Many students have difficulty finding campus buildings and obtaining information about facilities, services, operating hours, and campus regulations. Students often spend extra time searching for information because it is scattered across different sources.

## Main Current Workarounds

* Asking friends or senior students
* Asking university staff
* Searching the RSU website
* Searching social media pages
* Using Google Maps

## Strongest Evidence Found

* 12 out of 19 respondents reported having difficulty finding buildings or campus information during their first semester.
* 10 out of 19 respondents said they usually ask friends or senior students for help.
* 11 out of 19 respondents stated that current methods are time-consuming or inconvenient.

## Weak Evidence or Unclear Findings

* Only 8 respondents said they would definitely use an image-recognition campus assistant.
* Some respondents preferred traditional methods such as asking friends directly.
* More evidence is needed to validate specific MVP features.

## What We Must Revise

The team should focus the MVP on the most important student problems, particularly building identification and access to campus information, rather than implementing too many advanced features in the first version.

## Decision for Next Lab

* Continue with same problem direction.

## Updated Problem Statement

Freshmen and international students at Rangsit University frequently experience difficulty identifying campus buildings and finding accurate information about services, facilities, and operating hours. They currently rely on asking friends, staff, or searching multiple online sources, which is often inconvenient and time-consuming.
