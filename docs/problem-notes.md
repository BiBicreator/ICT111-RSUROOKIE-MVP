# Problem Notes For Lab-3

## Selected Case

RSU Campus Buddy (Smart Campus Chatbot for Student Services with Image Recognition)

|---|---|---:|---:|
| Freshmen (1st Year Students) | They are unfamiliar with campus buildings and services. | 10 | 5|

## Observed / Reported Problem

Many students have difficulty identifying campus buildings and finding information about facilities, services, operating hours, student clubs, and university regulations. Information is scattered across multiple sources, making it inconvenient and time-consuming to access.

## Current Alternatives or Workarounds

| Workaround | Who Uses It? | Weakness / Pain | Evidence Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| Asking friends or senior students | Freshmen and international students | Information may be inconsistent or unavailable. | Survey responses |
| Asking university staff | New students and visitors | Staff may not always be available. | Survey responses |
| Searching the RSU website | Most students | Information can be difficult to find and spread across different pages. | Survey responses |
| Searching social media pages | Most students | Information may be outdated or incomplete. | Survey responses |
| Using Google Maps | New students and visitors | Provides location only and lacks detailed campus information. | Survey responses |

## Repeated Pain Points

| Pain Point | Number of Mentions | Example Evidence | Severity |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Difficulty finding buildings and classrooms | | "I couldn't find my classroom during the first week." | High |
| Difficulty finding information about facilities and services | | "I didn't know where the student service office was." | High |

## Consequences

| Consequence | Example | Evidence Strength |
|---|---|---|
| Wasted time | Students spend extra time searching for information. | Medium |
| Stress and frustration | Students feel confused and anxious, especially during the first semester. | High |
| Arriving late to classes or activities | Students cannot locate buildings on time. | Medium |
| Dependence on other people | Students frequently ask friends or staff for assistance. | High |

## Notes for MVP Direction

The MVP should focus on helping students quickly identify campus buildings and access essential information in one place. Core features should include:

- Building image upload and identification
- Building information and facilities
- Operating hours and regulations
- Chatbot for campus-related questions
- Mobile-friendly and easy-to-use interface

Advanced features such as indoor navigation or multilingual support can be considered in future versions.
