# User Consent Statement

This prototype was developed as part of the **ICT105** course at Rangsit University for educational purposes. Participants are invited to test the **RSU Campus Buddy – Smart Campus Chatbot for Student Services with Image Recognition** and provide feedback on its usability and functionality.

Participation in this prototype evaluation is **voluntary**. Users may stop testing at any time. The prototype is intended only for learning, testing, and project improvement. Participants should **not upload or provide sensitive personal information**, such as student ID numbers, phone numbers, passwords, financial information, or personal documents.

## Data Collected

- Name, nickname, or anonymous participant ID (optional)
- User role (Student or Staff)
- Task completion result
- Time taken to complete tasks
- Feedback score (1–5)
- Interest level (1–5)
- Usability comments and suggestions
- General chatbot interaction data (non-personal)

## Data Use

The collected information will be used only for:

- Evaluating the usability of the prototype
- Improving the AI chatbot and user interface
- Analyzing customer validation results for the ICT105 project
- Preparing the final project report and presentation

The collected data will **not** be shared with third parties, sold, or used for commercial purposes. Any screenshots or reports included in project documentation will use anonymous or non-identifiable participant information whenever possible.

## Consent

By participating in the prototype evaluation, you confirm that:

- You understand the purpose of the prototype testing.
- Your participation is voluntary.
- You will not provide unnecessary personal or sensitive information.
- You agree that your anonymous feedback may be used to improve the prototype and for academic reporting within the ICT105 course.
